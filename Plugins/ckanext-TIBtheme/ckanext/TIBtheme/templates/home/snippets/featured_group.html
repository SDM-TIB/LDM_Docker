Federated Search

Retrieve answers from multiple ORKGs in a single query

  • This feature enables users to execute SPARQL queries over a federation of ORKGs, i.e., retrieving answers from multiple KGs with a single query. DeTrusty decomposes the query and retrieves the answers from the appropriate KGs. The federation includes but is not limited to the LDM KG and the ORKG.
